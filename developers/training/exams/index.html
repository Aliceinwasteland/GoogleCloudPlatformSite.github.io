Google Cloud Platform Training Exams

Google offers 5 Cloud Platform qualification web-based timed exams accessible from your camera-equipped computer.

Test your level of expertise and earn a Google Qualified Developer or Google Qualified Cloud Platform Developer designation by passing the Cloud Platform qualification exams online. You can take up to 5 exams, one for each Google Cloud technology. The exams cover material from the CP300 training package and challenge you to deeply explore additional resources.

To successfully take this exam, you must understand the fundamentals of each technology and be able to apply them to basic problems.

Qualification Exams

  • CP301: Google App Engine

    Duration: 90 minutes
    Topics: Basics, Architecture & Optimization, Managing Applications, Authentication, Datastore, Query & Transactions, Memcache, Queues & Cron

  • CP304: Google BigQuery

    Duration: 90 minutes
    Topics: Basics, Developer, Advanced

  • CP302: Google Cloud Storage

    Duration: 90 minutes
    Topics: Basics, App Engine Integration (API & Client Libraries), Backup & Archive, Security

  • CP305: Google Compute Engine

    Duration: 90 minutes
    Topics: Basics, Developer Concepts, Machines, Zones, Images, Instances, Disks,Networking, Services, Routing, API, Projects

  • CP303: Google Cloud SQL

    Duration: 60 minutes
    Topics: Basics, Administration, Interoperability, Product Specifics & Comparison

Passing any of these multiple-choice exams earns you the designation of a Google Qualified Developer for that specific product along with a certificate emailed to you. Passing 3 or more earns you the designation of a Google Qualified Cloud Platform Developer.

Proceed to https://www.webassessor.com/googlecloud to access these exams.
