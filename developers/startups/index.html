$100,000 in credit and 24/7 support for eligible startups.
Focus on building your product and don't worry about complex
infrastructure. Google manages your application, database,

Startup benefits

$100,000 free

Get $100,000 in Cloud Platform credit for 1 year.
Credit can be applied to all Cloud Platform products.

Office hours

Schedule 1:1 technical architecture reviews with Cloud Platform solutions engineers.

24/7 support

We offer 24/7 coverage, with phone support and rapid response times.

Eligibility

This offer is available to startups that meet the following criteria:

In an approved Accelerator, Incubator or VC fund

Haven't received previous Cloud Platform credits

Less than $5 million in funding

Less than $500,000 in annual revenue

To apply, contact your Accelerator, Incubator, or VC about Google Cloud Platform for Startups.
If they are not in the program, email CloudPlatformStartups@google.com to get them added.
