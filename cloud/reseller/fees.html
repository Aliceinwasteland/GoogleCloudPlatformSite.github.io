Fees

During the Google Cloud Platform Reseller Trusted Tester Program, participating Trusted Tester partners will receive information about the appropriate reseller prices for the Trusted Tester period from the Google staff managing this Trusted Tester program or from their respective Google Cloud partner manager. Such information may be verbal or via email.

Google will update the Google Cloud Platform Reseller Program Fees schedule at this same URL, or such updated URL as Google may determine and communicate, after the Trusted Tester program is complete and as Google moves to a general release of this Reseller Program.
